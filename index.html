About Us

A school robotics team is a group of students who design, build, and program robots to compete in competitions or work on engineering challenges. These teams participate in events like FIRST Robotics, VEX Robotics, and BattleBots, where they apply STEM skills (science, technology, engineering, and math) in real-world problem-solving. Members usually take on different roles, such as coding, mechanical design, strategy, or fundraising. Robotics teams help students develop teamwork, creativity, and technical skills—plus, they’re a great way to explore careers in engineering and technology.

Web Design

A school robotics team website is a great way to showcase your team's achievements, projects, and events. It helps attract new members, connect with sponsors, and keep the community updated. A good website should include sections like About the Team, Projects, Sponsors, Events, and Contact Info. You can build it using platforms like Wix, WordPress, or custom HTML & CSS. Keeping the design clean, easy to navigate, and mobile-friendly ensures a great user experience. Regular updates with photos, videos, and competition results will keep visitors engaged.
